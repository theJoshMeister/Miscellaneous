About

Joshua Long is the Chief Security Analyst for an international cybersecurity firm. He has been researching and battling cyber threats for more than 20 years. Josh is a regularly featured speaker at security and technology conferences.

Josh's security research has been acknowledged by Apple as well as the mainstream and technology press, including CBS News, CNET, Lifehacker, MacTech Magazine, Macworld, Naked Security, The Register, ZDNet, and more.
